Step 1:
Login to Payment Center Application (https://paymentcenter.qpay123.com)
Step 2:
Click on ‘Settings’ on top right corner of the application
Step 3:
Under Advanced Settings, type in your password and click ‘Unlock Settings’
Step 4:
Click ‘Manage Devices’ link and a new tab will open
Step 5:
To generate a request for a new certificate, click on ‘Add Branch Device’ button
Step 6:
Enter the email address, select the authentication option and click the ‘Add’ button
Step 7:
Open the email from the device where the certificate needs to be installed and copy/paste into mpos application
